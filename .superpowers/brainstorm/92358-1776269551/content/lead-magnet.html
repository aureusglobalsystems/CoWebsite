What you need to set this up
Tools (all free or near-free to start)
Mailchimp — free tier ConvertKit — free tier Formspree (already on site) Google Docs → PDF for the resource Notion for drafting emails
The website change is a single new section added to the homepage — an email capture form that sends to Mailchimp or ConvertKit, which then auto-delivers the PDF and queues the follow-up emails. No complex tech. The hardest part is writing the 20-question assessment — which takes one afternoon if you know your clients.
Why this works
95% of your visitors aren't ready to hire today. They're researching, building a business case, or waiting for budget. Without a lead magnet, they leave and you never know they existed. With one, you capture their email, deliver real value upfront, and stay in their inbox until the moment they're ready — at which point Aureus is the name they already trust.

The assessment idea works particularly well for B2B data & AI because it's genuinely useful (helps them think through their platform), positions you as the expert, and surfaces the exact pain points your sales conversation would anyway.
